Director's Message

Dear Reader,

Welcome to the Sir Syed Academy (a subsidiary of Einpirell). We are proud to have you with us and it will be our sincere endeavor to fulfill your objective.

In recent years interest in English has been growing interest not in the literature, but in the language, which has proved its utility in Indian society. It has now become clear that English is the lingua franca of the global economy and that, in order to be universally understood and accepted in any educational, social or commercial situation, knowledge of English is necessary. Apart from this, English language is the major medium of higher education in most Indian universities, especially in science, medicine, management and technology, which are the backbone of modern education through which national and international integration can be achieved. It is, therefore, necessary to have knowledge of both Spoken as well as Written English in order to make a contribution to every aspect of life. As soon as a person steps out of his own language group, the need for a link language comes up. This is a problem very peculiar to India because we are a multilingual society. Now a day a large number of students, doctors, engineers, businessmen, even housewives are finding it necessary to use English for effective communication. In order to fulfill these needs we offer a range of courses in spoken English that has been designed to strengthen and improve your career and personality too.

At Sir Syed Academy our mission is to provide an outstanding English Speaking skill and inspire our students to engage in both good communication and enriching personality. Our students set high expectations for themselves, enjoy learning spoken English.

If you wish to equip yourself adequately in this competitive world through development of your communication skills, leadership qualities and human values needed to face the challenges of any struggle, be it in India or anywhere in the world, Sir Syed Academy is the right place for you.

This is your first introduction to Sir Syed Academy. It offers information to help you take decision, which can beautify your future. your are welcome to approach our office staff for any information.

I sincerely hope that you will do us the honour of joining our family.
