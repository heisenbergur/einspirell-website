Director's Message

“Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known and learn.” – Carl Sagan

All of us know that the world is undergoing a process of turbulent change. There is an element of uncertainty & insecurity in the air. There is a challenge to migrate ourselves towards the world for wisdom with extraordinary knowledge and skills. I strongly believe that we all have that potential to create the change we wish to see in the world.

But this raises a question in our mind: HOW ? CAN WE? HOW CAN WE?

But no worries! Many of us face the same dilemma. Don’t forget the world is full of people like you, people gifted with passion and desire to make a difference, people who see a problem and look for a solution; and here I am to solve your problem. I’ll not only help you find the confidence to step up and make that difference, but also support you as you lead and live a life of great experiences.

The very basic unit for achieving such wonders is education. Education is not just about the subjects that are learnt and taught in school. It’s a lifelong exercise that can be unbelievably exciting if only one wishes to jump onto the train of experience and take a trip to every conceivable place on earth. Communication is another “must” for this experience.

Here’s the very well-known language ‘’ENGLISH’’ which has the capability to advance your career prospects, the potential to connect with fast changing contemporary culture and the opportunity to pursue interests outside of your homes, families, cities, and even countries. It advances you with great effects, creativity and self-growth. For one example, Every business with an international clientele uses English language as the prime source of communication. Knowing the language makes your life and experiences more interesting .

While education improves our life, English improves our life experiences. And I am here to add color to your life experiences. Einspirell in collaboration with Sir Syed Academy , is the right place which provides wings to achieve your dreams.

I often see candidates who want to give a tough competition but lack, not because of knowledge but the language to express in interviews, the corporate people who worked hard to build an empire but lack to communicate in the business world and our hardworking housewives who might come up as entrepreneurs but are limited to access for the language. This is where I thought to come up with the idea of disseminating knowledge and education of english language

In the present scenario of globalization, communicative English is playing a significant role to hone & polish by preparing one to face the challenges in career. Private and Public sector has awakened to the importance of English communication to survive in today’s competitive environment.

To make this journey of knowledge fruitful and seamless, Sir Syed Academy is providing you an E-learning platform which gives you an instant access for spoken English and learning program to engage each person by one’s interest for good and upgrading one by enriching of personality in communicative ways by providing one to one interactive sessions as you can learn to speak and communicate with confidence. One would be equipped with adequate expectations during the time of experiencing & learning with the help of imbibing life skill values, in addition to professional knowledge.

Change starts with a single step. I positively hope to be a part and connect to your journey in making a difference.

Happy experiencing!
