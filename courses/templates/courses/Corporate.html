Corporate training

Unlock your company's global potential. Equip your team with the real-world English skills to succeed anywhere through specialized private English tutoring from any device.

Why take this course?

Corporate training will help you excel in your workplace by teaching you the language skills you need to discuss specialized topics in business.

What will you be able to do?

This course covers 14 specialized topics in business such as marketing, e-commerce, and outsourcing. You will learn the vocabulary and grammatical structures needed to participate in discussions about each topic. The course focuses on simulating real-life business situations to build professional communicative competency. You will also learn to confidently talk about your own professional experiences and expertise.
