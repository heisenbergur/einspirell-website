Depression, Anxiety, OCD

Depression is a mood disorder that can affect person daily life. It may be described as feelings of low, sadness, loss, anger, Lack of interest in food, meeting people, too much sleep, not enjoying daily routine work at personal and professional level.
Anxiety is your body's natural response to stress. It's a feeling of fear or apprehension about what's to come.
Obsessive–compulsive disorder is a thought process problem in which a person feels the need to perform certain routines repeatedly or has certain thoughts repeatedly. The person is unable to control either the thoughts or activities for more than a short period of time.
